WELCOME TO CANADIA BANK PREMIER SERVICES

What is Canadia Bank Premier Services?

Canadia Bank Premier Services is an exclusive, one-stop service tailored to meet any of your banking needs. Experience personalized assistance from our dedicated and highly-trained Relationship Managers, comfortable space, and many more exclusive benefits, ensuring seamless banking services.

What are the privileges of being a Canadia Bank Premier Services Customer?

Dedicated Premier Services Lounges

Exclusive access to five (5) locations in Phnom Penh to handle your banking needs. Skip the line and enjoy comfort, privacy, free Wi-Fi, and complimentary beverages while waiting.

Exceptional one-stop service by dedicated staff

Our dedicated and highly-trained Relationship Managers provide proactive support, insights, and advice, and help you manage all your banking needs faster.

Special offerings on products and services

Take advantage of special offers on Canadia Bank's products and services.

Other exclusive privileges

Find a comfortable venue for business meetings at our Premier Services lounge. Additionally, enjoy exclusive offers from Canadia Group Companies and complimentary access to Plaza Premium Lounge at the airport globally for Infinite and Elite cardholders.

How can you enjoy the Premier Services:

  • Upgrade to or open an Elite Account or
  • Show either your Canadia Visa Infinite or Elite Card to our Relationship Manager or
  • Get any invitation from our Premier Services Relationship Managers.
